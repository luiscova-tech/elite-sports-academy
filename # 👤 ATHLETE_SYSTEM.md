# Purpose

Athletes are the heart of Elite Sports Academy.

Players should not simply recruit anonymous units.

Players should discover, develop, mentor, and celebrate athletes throughout long careers.

The goal is for players to remember people, not numbers.

Buildings create opportunities.

Coaches develop talent.

Athletes create stories.

---

# Design Philosophy

Elite Sports Academy is about building future Olympic champions.

Every athlete should feel unique.

The player should become emotionally attached to athletes over time.

The game should generate memorable stories rather than simply increasing statistics.

Version 1 does not support athlete transfers.

---

# Athlete Discovery

Players do not recruit athletes directly forever.

Instead, campuses develop local talent.

The player invests in:

Youth Development

Community Programs

Local Scouting

School Partnerships

Talent Identification

As these systems improve, new athletes are discovered.

Athlete generation should feel exciting.

Discovering an elite athlete should become a memorable moment.

# Athlete Peak

Athletes eventually reach their prime.

Peak years vary by athlete.

Elite athletes remain competitive longer.

Average athletes peak earlier.

Late bloomers develop more slowly.

---

# Athlete Retirement

Athletes eventually retire.

Retirement should never feel like losing progress.

Retired athletes become part of academy history.

Some exceptional athletes may enter the Hall of Fame.

# Athlete Ownership

Athletes belong to one country campus.

Players always know:

Where the athlete came from.

Who developed them.

Which campus they represent.

This strengthens campus identity.

---

# Olympic Qualification

Only the strongest athletes qualify.

Qualification should feel prestigious.

Not every athlete reaches the Olympics.

Winning Olympic medals should become one of the highest achievements in the game.

---

# Academy Legacy

Players should remember athletes years later.

The goal is to create emotional attachment.

Players should think:

"I remember when Haruto won Olympic Gold."

rather than

"My Swimming Center reached Level 42."
