# 🏛 Headquarters

> "The heart of the world's greatest Olympic training academy."

---

# Purpose

Headquarters is the permanent home of the player's organization.

It is **not** another country.

It is the strategic center that connects every academy branch around the world.

Players return to Headquarters to make long-term decisions affecting the entire organization rather than one specific campus.

---

# Design Philosophy

Countries answer:

> "How do I succeed here?"

Headquarters answers:

> "What kind of academy am I building?"

Everything inside Headquarters should strengthen the fantasy of leading an international Olympic training organization.

---

# Core Principles

Headquarters never resets.

Headquarters is always available.

Headquarters grows throughout the entire game.

Every improvement made at Headquarters benefits the organization as a whole.

---

# Academy Funding

Academy Funding represents the organization's operating budget.

Unlike Training Points, Academy Funding is global.

Academy Funding is generated from:

- Campus Contributions
- Sponsors
- Government Grants
- Private Donations
- International Success

Academy Funding is used only for global improvements.

# Academy Philosophy

Players eventually choose how their academy develops.

Possible philosophies include:

High Performance

Youth Development

International Expansion

Sports Science

Community Development

Only one philosophy may be active at a time.

Each philosophy changes long-term strategy.

---

# Relationship With Country Campuses

Headquarters does not contain:

Local athletes.

Local competitions.

Local Training Points.

Country facilities.

Instead, Headquarters supports every campus equally.

Campuses generate success.

Headquarters transforms that success into long-term organizational growth.

---

# Home Screen

When the player is at Headquarters, the Home screen becomes Headquarters.

When the player travels to Japan, Home becomes the Japan Campus.

When the player travels to Brazil, Home becomes the Brazil Campus.

Home always represents the player's current location.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Headquarters should eventually feel like the command center of a global sports organization.

Players should open Headquarters to make strategic decisions while country campuses remain focused on daily operations.

The player is never managing one country.

The player is leading one organization with many international campuses.
