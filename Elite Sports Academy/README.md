# 🏟 Elite Sports Academy

> **Build the world's greatest Olympic training academy.**

Elite Sports Academy is a mobile sports management game with idle progression where players start with a small local training center and grow it into an internationally recognized Olympic training academy.

Instead of simply collecting resources, players develop athletes, recruit coaches, expand facilities, travel the world, and prepare future champions through a long-term World Tour.

## 🌍 Vision

Elite Sports Academy is **not just an idle clicker.**

It is a sports management game with idle progression where players build a small academy into the world's greatest Olympic training center.

Every system is designed to reinforce the fantasy of developing athletes, expanding facilities, traveling the world, and creating future Olympic champions.

---

## 🧭 Development Philosophy

- 🏗 Build the Academy
- 👥 Develop Athletes and Coaches
- 🌍 Compete Around the World
