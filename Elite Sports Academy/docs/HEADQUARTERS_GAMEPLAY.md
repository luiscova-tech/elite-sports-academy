# Purpose

Headquarters is the player's permanent global base.

It is the primary destination for spending Academy Funding and improving the Global Academy.

Unlike campuses, Headquarters affects the entire organization.

---

# Headquarters Philosophy

Campuses improve athletes.

Headquarters improves the academy.

Players should constantly decide between:

- Investing locally
- Investing globally

---

# Primary Currency

Academy Funding

Generated by every campus.

Training Points are never spent here.

# Upgrade Philosophy

Headquarters upgrades are permanent.

They affect every current and future campus.

Players should never feel forced to upgrade Headquarters immediately.

Instead, Headquarters should become increasingly valuable as more campuses are unlocked.

---

# Operations Capacity

Players should feel that Headquarters becomes the brain of the Global Academy.

Headquarters should never replace campuses.
