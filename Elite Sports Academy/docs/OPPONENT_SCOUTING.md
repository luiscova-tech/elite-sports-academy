# 🔍 OPPONENT_SCOUTING.md

# Purpose

Defines how players gather information about upcoming opponents.

Preparation should be a meaningful part of competition.

---

# Philosophy

Players should rarely enter a match without knowing something about their opponent.

Better scouting reveals more information.

---

# Available Information

Every opponent displays:

- Country
- Current World Ranking
- Championship Stars
- Recent Results
- Current Form

---

# Scouting Levels

Basic

- World Ranking
- Championship Stars

Advanced

- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Preferred Strategy

Elite

- Athlete Quality
- Coaching Quality
- Research Focus
- Historical Performance

---

# Strength Categories

Possible strengths include:

- Offense
- Defense
- Speed
- Endurance
- Technique
- Teamwork
- Experience

---

# Match Preview

Before every match, display:

Your Country

VS

Opponent

World Ranking

Championship Stars

Win Probability

Strength Comparison

Recent Results

---

# Player Decisions

Scouting should influence:

- Research priorities
- Facility upgrades
- Coach assignments
- Athlete selection

---

# Goal

Scouting should reward preparation without guaranteeing victory.
