# 🏁 POST_COMPETITION_FLOW.md

# Purpose

Defines what happens immediately after every competition.

Every competition should provide useful feedback to the player.

---

# Results Screen

Displays:

- Final Placement
- Medal Won
- Opponent
- Final Score
- Championship Progress

---

# Rewards

Award:

- Training Points
- Academy Funding
- Reputation
- Experience

Rewards are shown individually before being added.

---

# Rankings

Update:

- World Ranking
- Continental Ranking
- Sport Ranking

Changes are highlighted.

Example

↑ Japan +2

↓

USA -1

---

# Championship History

If applicable:

- Champion
- Runner-up
- Third Place

The season history is updated immediately.

---

# Championship Stars

If a World Championship is won:

- Add one permanent star to the country's shield for that sport.
- Update the country's championship total.
- Record the championship year.

---

# Records

Check for:

- New World Record
- Longest Winning Streak
- Largest Upset
- Most Championships

New records are celebrated.

---

# News

Generate world headlines.

Examples

- Japan Wins Third Straight Baseball Championship
- Ghana Eliminates USA
- Slovenia Reaches #1 in Cycling

---

# Player Review

Before returning to the main menu, show:

- What went well
- What needs improvement
- Recommended next objective

Example

Strengths

- Pitching
- Defense

Weaknesses

- Batting
- Youth Development

Suggested Next Step

Research Advanced Batting.

---

# Goal

Every competition should answer one question:

"What should I do before my next competition?"
