# Purpose

Country Campuses are the operational branches of Elite Sports Academy.

Each campus represents a partnership with an existing national training center.

Players expand their academy internationally by opening new campuses.

Campuses never replace one another.

They become permanent parts of one global organization.

---

# Design Philosophy

Players do not start over.

Players expand.

Every campus should feel unique.

Every campus should teach different management decisions.

Every campus contributes to the success of the Global Academy.

# Coaches

Coaches belong to the Global Academy.

Players may assign coaches to any campus.

Coach transfers should become an important strategic decision.

Version 1 keeps transfers simple.

---

# Athletes

Athletes belong to one campus.

Athletes never transfer between campuses in Version 1.

Every campus develops local talent.

Every athlete represents their home country.

---

# Campus Development

Every campus begins with:

One existing training center.

One local coach.

A small group of athletes.

One core facility.

The player modernizes and expands the campus.

Players never begin with an empty lot.

---

# Sports Structure

Every campus contains:

## Two Core Sports

The country's strongest sports.

---

## Two Shared Sports

Olympic sports that connect progression across the world.

---

## One Wildcard Sport

A memorable sport unique to that country or region.

Every campus should immediately feel different.
