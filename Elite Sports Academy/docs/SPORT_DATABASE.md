# Purpose

This document is the master reference for every sport in Elite Sports Academy.

It defines:

- Sport category
- Competition circuits
- Countries where the sport appears
- Signature countries
- Olympic status

This document should remain the single source of truth for sports.

---

# Sport Categories

## Olympic Sports

Core sports that form the foundation of Elite Sports Academy.

## Regional Sports

Sports that appear primarily in specific regions.

## Exclusive Sports

Sports available in only one or a few countries.

## Future Sports

Reserved for later expansions.

# Core Design Rules

Regions determine where campuses are built.

Competition Circuits determine where sports are played.

A country belongs to only one region.

A country may participate in multiple Competition Circuits.
