# Design Rules

- Every country has one defining specialty.
- Every country has at least one signature sport.
- Global Icons should feel immediately powerful.
- Specialist campuses should excel in one area without becoming mandatory.

---

# Design Philosophy

Players should choose campuses because of their strengths, not because one country is objectively better than another.
