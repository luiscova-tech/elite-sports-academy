# 🔄 GAME_LOOP.md

# Purpose

Defines the complete gameplay loop from the player's perspective.

Every major system should support this loop.

---

# Core Loop

Choose a Country

↓

Choose a Sport

↓

Develop the Program

↓

Recruit Athletes

↓

Train Athletes

↓

Research Improvements

↓

Compete

↓

Analyze Results

↓

Upgrade the Program

↓

Advance to the Next Season

↓

Repeat

---

# Long-Term Loop

Develop Programs

↓

Win Regional Championships

↓

Qualify for World Championships

↓

Win World Championships

↓

Earn Championship Stars

↓

Build a Dynasty

↓

Become a Sporting Legend

---

# Player Decisions

Every season the player chooses:

- Which sport to prioritize
- Which facilities to improve
- Which research to complete
- Which athletes to recruit
- Which competitions to enter

Resources should never allow every option.

---

# Win Condition

There is no final ending.

Success is measured by the legacy created.

Examples:

- Most World Championships
- Most Olympic Gold Medals
- Greatest Dynasty
- Highest World Ranking
- Most Decorated Country

---

# The Living World

While the player develops their nation:

- AI countries improve
- Rivalries form
- Rankings change
- Records are broken
- Dynasties rise and fall
- History is written

The world never waits for the player.
