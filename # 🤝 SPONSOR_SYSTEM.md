# Purpose

Sponsors provide long-term partnerships that help Elite Sports Academy grow into the world's leading Olympic training organization.

Sponsors are not simply sources of money.

Sponsors should create strategic decisions.

Players should feel proud to earn partnerships with world-class organizations.

---

# Design Philosophy

Sponsors should be earned.

Not purchased.

Every sponsor represents recognition that the academy has achieved a certain level of excellence.

Different sponsors should support different academy philosophies.

Players should choose sponsors based on strategy rather than simply selecting the highest reward.
