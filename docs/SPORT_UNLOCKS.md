# Design Rules

- Every campus should introduce at least one new sport.
- Signature sports should debut with their home campus.
- Global icons should unlock iconic sports early.
- Niche sports should provide long-term goals.

---

# Design Philosophy

Unlocking a new sport should feel as exciting as unlocking a new campus.
