# 📅 SEASON_ENGINE.md

# Purpose

Defines how one in-game season progresses.

The Season Engine drives every major gameplay system.

---

# Philosophy

The player should always feel that the world is moving.

Every season should create:

- New champions
- New rankings
- New rivalries
- New history

---

# Annual Flow

Preseason

↓

Training

↓

Research

↓

Regional Competitions

↓

World Competitions

↓

Season Awards

↓

Offseason

↓

Next Season

---

# Preseason

Players decide:

- Research priorities
- Facility upgrades
- Coach assignments
- Athlete recruitment

AI countries also make improvements.

---

# Training Phase

Athletes develop.

Facilities produce improvements.

Research progresses.

Youth athletes emerge.

---

# Regional Season

Regional championships begin.

Countries attempt to qualify for major events.

World rankings begin changing.

---

# World Season

Major championships occur.

Examples:

- World Championships
- Olympic Games
- Sport World Championships

Championships update:

- History
- Rankings
- Stars
- Records

---

# Awards Season

Every sport presents annual awards.

Hall of Fame candidates are evaluated.

Historical records are updated.

---

# Offseason

Players review:

- Performance
- Finances
- Research
- Rankings

Planning begins for the next season.

---

# Living World

The AI completes every season using the same systems as the player.

Every country:

- Trains
- Improves
- Competes
- Wins
- Loses
- Evolves

No season should ever feel identical.
