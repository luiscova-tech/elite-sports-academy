# Purpose

This document defines the annual competition calendar for Elite Sports Academy.

It determines when regional events, world championships, and Olympic competitions occur.

The objective is to create meaningful long-term planning instead of isolated competitions.

---

# Design Principles

Competitions should feel like real sporting seasons.

Players should prepare months or years in advance for major events.

Not every competition should occur every year.

Different sports should have their own competitive rhythm.

---

# Competition Levels

## Regional

Regional Championships

Held every year.

Examples:

North America & Caribbean Championships

South American Championships

European Championships

Asian Championships

African Championships

Oceania Championships

---

## Global

World Championships

Held every year.

Only qualified athletes participate.

---

## Sport Championships

Held every two years.

Examples:

Football World Championship

World Baseball Classic

Basketball World Cup

Rugby World Championship

Cycling World Championship

Swimming World Championship

Athletics World Championship

Combat Sport World Championships

Future Winter World Championships

---

## Olympic Games

Held every four years.

Highest prestige.

Highest rewards.

Represents the pinnacle of Version 1 progression.

---

## Winter Olympic Games

Future expansion.

Held every four years.

Offset from the Summer Olympic Games.

# Design Philosophy

Players should prepare for competitions rather than simply reacting to them.

The calendar should create anticipation.

Winning an Olympic Gold Medal should feel like the culmination of years of planning.

Major competitions should define the rhythm of the entire academy.
