# 🧬 SPORT_PROGRAMS.md

# Purpose

Defines how every country develops each individual sport.

Each sport has its own independent program.

---

# Philosophy

Countries are not simply "good" or "bad."

They are the result of decades of investment.

Every sport evolves independently.

---

# Program Categories

Every sport contains five development areas.

- Facilities
- Coaching
- Youth Development
- Sports Science
- Tradition

---

# Facilities

Represents infrastructure.

Examples

- Stadiums
- Pools
- Training Centers
- Equipment

Higher facilities improve athlete development.

---

# Coaching

Represents coaching quality.

Improves:

- Training efficiency
- Tactical decisions
- Athlete progression

---

# Youth Development

Represents future talent.

Higher values produce stronger young athletes.

Youth systems create long-term success.

---

# Sports Science

Represents research and innovation.

Improves:

- Recovery
- Injury Prevention
- Training Quality
- Performance

---

# Tradition

Represents cultural popularity.

Tradition changes very slowly.

Countries naturally invest more in sports with strong tradition.

Examples

Japan

Baseball
★★★★★

Football
★★★

Rugby
★★

---

# Player Control

The player chooses where to invest.

Examples

Develop Ghana Baseball.

Ignore Ghana Swimming.

Transform India Cycling.

Build Puerto Rico Athletics.

---

# AI Control

AI countries follow the same system.

Every offseason they decide where to invest.

No country improves every sport equally.

---

# Goal

Every country's sporting identity should evolve naturally over decades.
