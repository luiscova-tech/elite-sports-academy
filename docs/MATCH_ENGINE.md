# ⚔️ MATCH_ENGINE.md

# Purpose

Defines how match outcomes are determined.

Matches should reward preparation rather than randomness.

---

# Match Score

Every match is influenced by:

- Athlete Quality
- Coaching
- Facilities
- Research
- Team Chemistry
- Current Form
- Home Advantage (when applicable)

Randomness should exist but never dominate.

---

# Match Phases

↓

↓

Statistics

---

# Team Strength

Every country receives a match rating based on:

- Athlete Rating
- Coach Rating
- Research Bonuses
- Facility Bonuses
- Temporary Form

---

# Upsets

Upsets are always possible.

However, stronger teams should win most of the time.

Historic upsets are recorded permanently.

---

# Match Statistics

Examples

- Possession
- Shots
- Errors
- Successful Plays
- Efficiency

Displayed statistics depend on the sport.

---

# Injuries

Some competitions may result in injuries.

Recovery depends on:

- Sports Science
- Recovery Facilities
- Medical Research

---

# Momentum

Winning increases confidence.

Losing may temporarily reduce confidence.

Momentum should never permanently determine outcomes.

---

# Goal

The better-prepared country should usually win.

Unexpected victories should become memorable moments.
