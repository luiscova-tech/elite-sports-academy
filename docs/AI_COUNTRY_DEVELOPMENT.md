# 🤖 AI_COUNTRY_DEVELOPMENT.md

# Purpose

Defines how AI-controlled countries improve over time.

The AI should create a believable and competitive sports world without feeling unfair.

---

# Philosophy

Every country follows the same core rules as the player.

The AI does not receive hidden advantages.

Major sports nations begin with stronger programs, but every nation can improve.

---

# National Programs

Each country develops every sport independently.

Each sport has its own:

- Facilities
- Coaching
- Youth Development
- Sports Science
- Competitive Experience

Example

Japan Baseball improves independently from Japan Football.

---

# Annual Decisions

Every offseason, AI countries decide where to invest.

Possible priorities:

- Facilities
- Research
- Youth Development
- Coaching
- Athlete Recruitment

Countries should not improve every area equally.

---

# National Identity

Countries prefer investing in their traditional sports.

Examples

Japan → Baseball

Brazil → Football

Kenya → Distance Running

Slovenia → Cycling

However, countries may slowly expand into new sports over time.

---

# Growth

Every country has a limited annual budget.

Strong countries grow steadily.

Smaller countries can experience rapid growth after successful tournaments.

---

# Decline

Dominant countries can decline.

Possible causes:

- Aging athletes
- Poor investment
- Coaching turnover
- Lack of recent success

Dynasties eventually end.

---

# Player Influence

The player's success changes the world.

Other countries respond by increasing investment in sports where the player becomes dominant.

---

# Goal

The AI should create believable long-term competition.

Every save should produce a different sporting world.
